These Hex codes are subject to change and are unique to every remote and TV. Please use Arduino/NodeMCU to decode the codes for your own remote.

40BFA05F

STB Power:
11D9D02F

11D9CE31

Channel Down:
11D92ED1

Volume Up:
11D98E71

Volume Down:
11D94EB1
